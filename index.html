From the beginning, we've has ALWAYS cleaned & disinfected our equipments.

Hi everyone, this is Bradford. Now more than ever it's important that all of you know; from the beginning 15 years ago to the present BMS has always steam cleaned our Durable Medical Rental Equipment (DMRE) before it is delivered to you. Our cleaning procedures have been updated to include required Center for Disease Control (CDC) Cleaning and Disinfection Guidelines. Going forward, prior to delivery all DMRE shall meet or exceed those requirements by disinfection with 70% isopropyl alcohol (IPA) and/or dilute bleach solution.
